Training

Emidio Capriotti is actively promoting training in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. In collaboration with other researchers, we are working to develop a training program in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (CB2) at University of Bologna. Students with research interests in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics please contact Emidio Capriotti.

From March 13, 2018 I will give the Module 2 of the Laboratory of Bioinformatics I course at the Master of Bioinformatics at the University of Bologna.

  • CB2-201 Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (3 Credit). Instructors: Malay Basu and Emidio Capriotti
